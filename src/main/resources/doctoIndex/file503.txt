Below is the list of products that will saturate your body and maintain energy throughout the day. In addition, each of the products is very healthy. 

• Apple: contains a large number of vitamins and minerals, besides apples are very low-calorie. 

• Banana: excellent source of potassium, which helps to adjust the cardiac activity. 

• Bell Pepper: rich in antioxidants and vitamins A and C. 

• Dark chocolate: a great alternative to high-calorie desserts. 

• Carrot: an excellent source of fiber, which helps to fight hunger. 

• Green Smoothies: filled with lots of vitamins, a great charge for the whole day. 

• Blueberries: a great way to improve brain function. 

• Avocado: helps to lower cholesterol level. 

• Dried fruits: a perfect snack, but keep in mind that they are very high in calories. 

• Salad: fills up your body not only with minerals and vitamins, but also energizes and satisfies your hunger. 

Eat healthy and always be in the shape.
