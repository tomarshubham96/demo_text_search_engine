5 BEST WASHINGTON MUSEUMS
WRITTEN BY M. PETROVA CATEGORY: TRAVEL AND RECREATION

There are more than 60 museums in Washington, but we have chosen TOP-5 for you, they are considered to be famous and popular. 

National Museum of Natural History is one of the most famous museums in Washington and is considered to be a part of the Smithsonian Institution - the largest museum and research complex in the world. Its collections contain over 125 million natural science specimens and cultural artifacts. 

The Natural History Museum is interesting not
only for children but also for adults. Here you will find the skeletons of dinosaurs, a huge collection of natural gemstones and minerals, exhibits about the life of primitive man, insect zoo, and much more. 

Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum offers visitors the opportunity to enjoy the air and space trips, the museum tells visitors about the unknown facts from the history of aeronautics, displaying hundreds of artifacts in 22 galleries. The museum has the largest collection of aviation and space technology in the world. 

Holocaust Memorial Museum is a monument to the millions of Jews who perished in the Nazi regime during World War II. A visit to this museum will have a great impact on you. Be sure you and your kids are ready to know horrific facts about those times. It goes without saying that this museum is worth visiting. 

National Gallery of Art is popular among museum goers. Many of the items were given to the museum exposition by famous people and collectors. Here you will find the works of famous artists such as Raphael, Rembrandt, Titian, and Van Dyke. The National Gallery in Washington, DC has one of the best and richest collections of art in the world.
